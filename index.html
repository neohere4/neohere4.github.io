About Us

NRI MedHub is a platform that connects non-resident Indians (NRIs) visiting India with top-rated doctors and medical facilities. Our mission is to provide a seamless, efficient, and reliable service that gives NRIs peace of mind and ensures they receive the best medical care and related services possible.

Our Services

Schedule Appointment

We streamline the process of scheduling appointments with top-rated doctors and medical facilities in India.

Medical Information

We provide comprehensive medical information to NRIs, including information about doctors, hospitals, and treatments.

Medical Coordination

We assist NRIs in coordinating their medical treatments in India, including arranging for transportation, accommodation, and aftercare services, to ensure continuity of care.
